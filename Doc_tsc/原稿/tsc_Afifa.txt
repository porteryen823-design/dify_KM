Question 1:

Note that standby points A, B, and C are all aligned on the same horizontal line since they share Y=1.
Vehicle 2 is currently at standby point B and needs to travel to the charger at (2,5). Meanwhile, Vehicle 1 has just finished charging and is on its way back to standby point A at (-4,1). Along the path ranging from (2,0) to (-4,0), there is a possibility that both vehicles will encounter each other, since both will be using that route, Vehicle 1 to return to its standby point and Vehicle 2 to reach the charger.
The question is: Should Vehicle 1 be given priority to pass through first and proceed to its standby point at (-4,1)? Or should Vehicle 2 move first to standby point C, then Vehicle 1 proceeds to standby point A, and finally Vehicle 2 continues its journey to the charger?
